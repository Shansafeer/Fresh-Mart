FRESH MART DAIRY PRODUCTS

The Fresh mart dairy section is stocked with a wide array of fresh, high-quality products. In addition to standard whole and skimmed milk, you'll find fortified options like vitamin D-enriched or lactose-free milk for those with specific nutritional needs. The yogurt range includes plain, Greek-style, and fruit-flavored varieties, each offering a unique texture and taste profile. Cheese enthusiasts can explore a selection of hard and soft cheeses, such as cheddar, mozzarella, brie, and blue cheese, sourced from both local and international producers. For baking and cooking, we carry unsalted and salted butter, as well as specialty butters like ghee and cultured butter. Complementing these are dairy-based spreads, whipped creams, and a variety of plant-based alternatives like almond and oat milk to accommodate customers with dietary preferences.
